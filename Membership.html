How to become a Member of Semirara Multi-Purpose Cooperative

Qualification for Membership

  • The membership of this Cooperative is open to all natueal persons, Filipino citizen, of legal age, with capacity to contract and, within the common bond and field of membership described as follows: RESIDENTIAL

    Regular Member:

    Regular employee of Semirara Mining and Power Corporation (SMPC) and its subsidiaries namely DMC-Construction Equipment Resources, Inc.(DMC-CERE), Divine Word School of Semirara Island, Inc. (DWSSII), and Semirara Training Center,Inc.(STCI), and are residing within the area of operation as defined and delimited in Article VI of the Articles of Cooperation thereof.
    Regular Employee of Semirara Multi-Purpose Cooperative (SEMCO)
    Regular member of Semirara Multi-Purpose Cooperative (SEMCO) who was seperated from employment that continues his/her membership in SEMCO, and are still residing Semirara Island.
    Probitionary or contractual employee of Semirara Multi-Purpose Cooperative (SEMCO),
    SMPC sub-contractor, Employee of SMPC supplier, Public or Private School Teacher, Who are residing within the area of operation as defined and delimited in Article VI of the Articles of Cooperative thereof,
    Regular member of Semirara Multi-Purpose Cooperative (SEMCO) who previously terminated his/her membership and are still residing and working in Semirara Island. Non-Teaching Staff of a Public or Private School; Operator=Member of SEMTODA only; Market Vendor; Business Owner; and is residing within the area of operation as defined and delimited in Article VI of the Articles of Cooperation therof.

    Associate members who meet the minimum requirements of regular membership, continues to patronize the Cooperative for two (2) years and signifies his/her intention to remain as member shall be considered a regular member.


    Requirements for Membership :

    A member must have a complied with the following requirements:

    Approved application for membership Certificate of completion of the prescribed Pre-Membership Education Seminar (PMES) Subcribed and paid the required minimum share capital and membership fee; and
    Patronize services of the Cooperative

    Application for Membership :

    An applicant for membership shall file a duly accomplished form to the Board of Directors who shall act upon the application within seven (7) days from the date of filling. The Board of Directors shall advice a form for the purpose which shall, aside from the personal data of the applicant, include the duties of a member to participate in all programs including but not limited to capital build-up and savings mobilization of the Cooperative and, such other information as may be deemed necessary.


    The application form for membership shall include an undertaking to uphold the by-laws, policies, guidelines, rules and regulations promulgated by the Board of Directors and the General Assembly. No application for membership shall be given due coures if not accompanied with a membership fee of One Hundred Pesos (Php100.00), which shall be refunded to the application in case of rejection.

    Appeal :

    An applicant whose application was denied by the Board of Directors may appeal to the General Assembly bby giving notice to the secretary of the Cooperative within thirty (30) days before the next General Assembly Meeting, whose decision on the matter shall be final.

    Minimum Share Capital Reguirements :

    An applicant for membership shall subscride at least one hundred fifty (50) shares and pay the value of atleast five(5) shares upon approval of his/her membership.
